   This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-3 effective
   2012-02-06, reclassifying the ISO 639-3 code element 'ori' (Oriya,
   which is represented by ISO 639-1 'or' in the Language Subtag
   Registry) as a macrolanguage encompassing 'ory' (Oriya, individual
   language) and 'spv' (Sambalpuri, Kosli).

   For more information on the ISO 639-3 change, refer to
   http://www.sil.org/iso639-3/cr_files/639-3_ChangeRequests_2011_Summary.pdf .
