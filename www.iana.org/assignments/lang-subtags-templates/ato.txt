This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-3 effective
2015-04-06, changing the reference name for 'ato' from "Atong" to "Atong
(Cameroon)" to distinguish it from "Atong (India)". This change
originally appeared in ISO 639-3 data files dated 2015-05-05, and was
not listed in the Summary of Outcomes document for any change series.
