This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-3 effective
2015-01-12, merging 'gfx' for "Mangetti Dune !Xung", 'mwj' for "Maligo",
and 'oun' for "!O!ung" into 'vaj'; changing the reference name for 'vaj'
from "Vasekela Bushman" to "Sekele"; and adding the additional names
"Northwestern !Kung" and "Vasekele".

For more information on the ISO 639-3 change, refer to:
http://www-01.sil.org/iso639-3/cr_files/639-3_ChangeRequests_2014_Summary.pdf
http://www-01.sil.org/iso639-3/cr_files/PastComments/CR_Comments_2014-057.pdf
