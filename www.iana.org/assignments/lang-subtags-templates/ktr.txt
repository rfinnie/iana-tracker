This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-3 effective
2016-01-15, merging 'ktr' for Kota Marudu Tinagas, 'kzj' for Coastal
Kadazan, 'kzt' for Tambunan Dusun, and 'tdu' for Tempasuk Dusun into
'dtp', adding "Kadazan Dusun" as the reference name for 'dtp', and
retaining "Central Dusun" as an additional name.

For more information on the ISO 639-3 change, refer to:
http://www-01.sil.org/iso639-3/cr_files/639-3_ChangeRequests_2015_Summary.pdf
