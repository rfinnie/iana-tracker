This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-3 effective
2014-02-03, restoring "Dhangu" as the reference name as it was before
being changed in 2013, and retaining "Djangu" as an additional name.

This change was not listed in the Series 2013 Summary of Outcomes
document. It was described in a document located at
http://www-01.sil.org/iso639-3/cr_files/PastComments/CR_Comments_2013-025.pdf
which explains a related decision.
