This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-3 effective
2021-01-22, splitting the code element 'wra' for Warapu into three new
code elements, 'bpe' (Bauni) , 'suo' (Bouni) and 'uni' (Uni).

For more information on the ISO 639-3 change, refer to
https://iso639-3.sil.org/sites/iso639-3/files/reports/639-3_ChangeRequests_2020_Summary.pdf
