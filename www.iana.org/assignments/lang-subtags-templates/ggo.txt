This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-3 effective
2016-01-15, splitting the code element 'ggo' for Southern Gondi into two
new code elements, 'esg' (Aheri Gondi) and 'wsg' (Adilabad Gondi), and
removing the Macrolanguage assignment of 'gon' from 'ggo' and adding it
to 'esg' and 'wsg'.

For more information on the ISO 639-3 change, refer to
http://www-01.sil.org/iso639-3/cr_files/639-3_ChangeRequests_2015_Summary.pdf
