   4. Intended meaning of the subtag:
      Tunumiit oraasiat is a variety of Kalaallisut (Greenlandic) spoken by
      the Tunumiit people in East Greenland. Part of the variation lies in
      polysynthesis being constructed differently between Tunumiisiut and
      Kalaallisut.
