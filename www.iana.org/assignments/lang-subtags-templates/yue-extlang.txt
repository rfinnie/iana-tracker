This registration adds the additional Description field "Cantonese" to
the existing extended language subtag 'yue'. Cantonese is the prestige
dialect of Yue, spoken in Guangzhou, Hong Kong, and Macao. This subtag
continues to represent all dialects within the Yue branch.
